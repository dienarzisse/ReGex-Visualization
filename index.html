[abc] any of a, b, or c
[^abc] not of a, b, or c
[a-g] character between a & g
Anchors
^abc$ start / end of the string
\b \B word, not-word boundary

Groups & Lookaround
(abc) capture group
\1 backreference to group #1
(?:abc) non-capturing group
(?=abc) positive lookahead
(?!abc) negative lookahead
Quantifiers & Alternation
a* a+ a? 0 or more, 1 or more, 0 or 1
a{5} a{2,} xactly five, two or more
a{1,3} between one & three
a+? a{2,}? match as few as possible
ab|cd match ab or cd

Multiline

Beginning/ending anchors (^/$) wil match the start/end of a line.
Singleline/Dotall

Dot (.) will match any character, including new line.

Warring!

The "dotall" feauture may not be supported in all browsers.
